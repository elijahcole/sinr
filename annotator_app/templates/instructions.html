How to Use iNatAtor:

  1. Select Taxonomy:
    • Start typing the taxonomy name in the input field.
    • Choose the correct taxonomy name from the dropdown list. Note: Only taxa from this list can be annotated.
  2. Generate Prediction:
    • Click the "Generate Prediction" button to view the current range prediction of the model for the selected taxa on the map.
  3. Map Views:
    • In the top right corner of the map, you can choose the map view:
      • World Light Gray Base
      • World Imagery
      • World Topo Map
      • OpenStreetMap
  4. Prediction Representation:
    • From the same menu, you can select how the prediction is represented on the map:
      • Heat Map
      • Polygons
      • Hexagons
    • You can also hide the current and previous annotations from this menu.
  5. Map Legend and Controls:
    • The map legend shows what the colors mean on the map.
    • Use map controls to zoom in and out of the map.
  6. Annotating Predictions:
    • Choose one of the drawing tools from the left menu to annotate predictions:
      • Polygon
      • Rectangle
    • Draw polygons or rectangles where you think the model's prediction is incorrect.
  7. Using Drawing Tools:
    • Polygon Tool:
      • Click to place the next corner of your polygon.
      • Continue placing corners to outline the area.
      • Click on the starting point to finish the polygon.
    • Rectangle Tool:
      • Click and drag to draw a rectangle.
  8. Editing Annotations:
    • To erase a polygon, choose the delete tool (trash icon) and click on the polygon.
    • Click the "Save Annotation" button to save your changes.
    • To restart the annotation process, click the "Clear Annotation" button.
  9. Saving Annotations:
    • After finishing your annotations, click the "Save Annotation" button.

Happy Annotating!
